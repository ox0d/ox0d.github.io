Dear everyone,

Once again, we would like to thank you for your interest in ICASEE-2022 conference. We would like to share the following notes with you regarding the conference.

  1. The registration desk will be at the entrance of the Education Faculty Building.
  2. Please confirm/make your registrations to the registration desk.
  3. Please do receive your badge and lunch card from the registration desk.
  4. You can take a copy of the Conference Abstract Book from the registration desk.
  5. Your presentation shall not exceed 15 minutes including Question and Answer. So please prepare your presentation accordingly.
  6. The conference program will be on all the hall doors.
  7. Please do take note of the time of your presentation.
  8. Please check the halls carefully from the conference program.
  9. All presentations online or on-campus will be video-recorded.
  10. If you'll join from Zoom, please rename it as your Full Name.

Looking forward to seeing you all at the conference.
