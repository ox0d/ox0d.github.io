Call for Paper

We biannually organize the international conference on “Applied Sciences, Energy and Environment”, ICASEE at Faculty of Education, Tishk International University, and Collage of Science, Salahaddin University Erbil, Erbil-IRAQ. The 7th ICASEE conference will be held on 25-27 June 2024.

The ICASEE 2024 is dedicated to offering a unique opportunity for researchers in the region, Iraq, and all over the world to gather, discuss, and collaborate within themselves and with international participants. The main theme of the conference is to embrace scientific research and value humanity's lives. Aiming to apply science to serving the community, providing them with better health, a better environment, and cleaner energy.

Herewith, prospective authors are kindly encouraged to contribute to and help shape the conference through submissions of their full-length research articles.

The conference program will include international workshops, prominent keynote speakers, poster sessions, and paper presentations in panel sessions.

Author Guidelines

Please refer to the conference website author guidelines. for the aim, author guidelines, Important Dates, and other information.

Publication

The ICASEE-2022 has paired up with the Journal of Science and Engineering (EAJSE) journal of Tishk International University and ZANCO Journal of Pure and Applied Sciences (ZJPAS) journal of Salahaddin University Erbil regarding publications of the conference. The selected papers will be considered for publication in the EAJSE and ZJPAS journals.

For more details about the journals, you can visit the webpage of EAJSE by the link https://eajse.tiu.edu.iq/submit/, and for the ZJPAS by the link https://zancojournal.su.edu.krd/index.php/JPAS/index.

All submitted papers will be subjected to double-blind peer review and critical evaluation by the conference scientific committee. Acceptance decisions will consider the paper's novelty, technical depth, and relevance to the conference scope.

We look forward to welcoming you at the ICASEE-2024 conference in Erbil, Kurdistan Region-Iraq.
