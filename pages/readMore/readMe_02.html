Prof. Marwan Suleiman Mousa

Prof. Marwan Suleiman Mousa is a full professor at the Department of Physics at Mutah University, Muâ€™tah, Jordan, and currently, the Vice President for Research, Planning and International Relations.

Prof. Mousa holds several prestigious awards; a two times Fulbright Fellowship 1991/1992 and 1999/2000, Alexander von Humboldt lifetime fellowship and many more. Prof. Mousa is the director of specialized Surface Physics and Materials Technology Lab., has over than 120 publications (inc. 104 Scopus papers) in the field of electron emission and other Surface Physics phenomena. He is currently, the President of the Jordanian Club of Humboldt Fellows, President of the Jordanian Physics Society, Elected Fellow of the Institute of Physics-UK, Fellow of the Int. Field Emission Society and Academician of the Euro Mediterranean Academy of Arts and Sciences (EMAAS).
