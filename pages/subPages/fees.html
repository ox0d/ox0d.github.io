Registration and Payment

All conference registration fees are in USD.

Registration Fees and Details
Registrations Fees Deadlines Explanation
Conference Early Registration-full paper submission $100 01.04.2024 Early submitted papers will be quickly evaluated for rapid publication before the conference.

Notes

  • Registration payment will include:
    1. Admission to scientific sessions
    2. Conference accessories
    3. Conference Participation Badge
    4. Certificate of attendance
    5. Coffee breaks
    6. Lunch
  • All conference papers *MUST* have one registered author
  • Please note that the paid registration fee cannot be refunded if you cannot participate in the conference.
  • For all accepted papers, the registration fee needs to be paid. Please note that your paper WILL NOT be published if the fee is not received.

Registration

All paid participants must fill the registration form.

Note: We will be issuing your certificate on the name that you have provided in the registration form. Hence, it is important that you provide us with the precise details of your information when you are completing the registration form.

Payment

To pay the registration fee, you can choose one of the following options:

  • You can make the payment at our accounting office at Tishk Internation University. Send a scanned copy of your payment (transfer or receipt) to icasee@tiu.edu.iq
  • You can make your payment through the Western Union, to the name Harmand Ali Hama
  • You can make payment to the registration desk during the conference time.

Address: Tishk International University, 100 mt. Street, near Filkey Baz (Square), 44001, Erbil, Iraq
